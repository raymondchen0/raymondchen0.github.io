Repetition

Branding, UI, Fitness App

Get in shape, stay motivated with the help of your friends and family. Getting that extra nudge from a gym buddy just got easier.

Overview

Repetition is a social fitness app. It aims to help bring together your friends into a central hub. By focusing on an overall community while also tracking more than just fitness it helps keep you and your friends motivated on your workout journey.

Progress and tracking of current workout
Messaging your gym buddy

Audience

I wanted the imagery and colors to target a more lively, younger audience. I used green because I did not want anything that was too agressive. I wanted people to stay calm and enjoy their workouts. The imagery also targets an overall younger demographic.

Conclusion

Repetition aims to be a motivator. Sometimes you just need a little push to start your journey. The vibrant green and numerous slogans help convey a sense of achievement. Reptition believes that anyone can begin their workout journey and achieve their unique set goals. Naturally, why not bring someone else along on your journey?
